<p><p>Spray Polyurethane Foam (SPF) is a versatile insulation material used in construction and industrial applications. It is categorized into two main types: open cell and closed cell. Open cell SPF is lightweight, flexible, and offers excellent sound absorption, making it ideal for interior applications. Closed cell SPF is denser, providing superior insulation and moisture resistance, suitable for exterior and demanding environments. Additionally, other types may include specialty formulations for specific applications, enhancing their functionality and performance.</p></p>
