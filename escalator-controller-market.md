<p><ul><li>Non-proprietary Escalator Controllers</li><li>Proprietary Escalator Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The escalator controller market is categorized into non-proprietary and proprietary controllers. Non-proprietary escalator controllers are open systems that allow compatibility with various manufacturers, promoting flexibility and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, proprietary escalator controllers are custom-built systems developed by specific manufacturers, often offering specialized features and optimized performance but locking users into a single vendor. The choice between these types affects maintenance options, upgrade paths, and overall system integration within broader building management frameworks.</p></p>
