<p><strong>The Vehicle Supercharger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roots Supercharger</li><li>Twin-Screw Supercharger</li><li>Centrifugal Supercharger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle supercharger market consists of three main types: Roots superchargers, twin-screw superchargers, and centrifugal superchargers. Roots superchargers provide immediate power boost with fixed displacement, often used in high-performance applications. Twin-screw superchargers offer higher efficiency and boost pressure through a helical compression mechanism, making them popular for modern engines. Centrifugal superchargers utilize a rotating impeller to compress air, delivering a power increase at higher RPMs, ideal for performance and racing applications. Each type serves distinct performance requirements in vehicles.</p></p>
