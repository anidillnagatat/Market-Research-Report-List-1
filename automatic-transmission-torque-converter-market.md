<p><p>The Automatic Transmission Torque Converter market comprises two main types: single-stage and multistage torque converters. Single-stage torque converters are designed with a single set of turbine and impeller, providing efficient power transfer and simplicity, ideal for low to moderate performance vehicles. In contrast, multistage torque converters feature multiple turbine stages, enhancing performance and torque multiplication, suitable for high-performance applications. Each type caters to specific vehicle needs, balancing efficiency and power delivery in automatic transmission systems.</p></p>
