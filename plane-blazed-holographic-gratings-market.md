<p><p>Plane Blazed Holographic Gratings are optical components used for diffracting light, with two primary market types: reflection gratings and transmission gratings. Reflection gratings collect and reflect light at specific angles, making them suitable for applications such as spectrometers and laser systems. In contrast, transmission gratings allow light to pass through while dispersing it, often used in imaging systems and wavelength selectors. Both types serve to manipulate light for various scientific, industrial, and telecommunications applications.</p></p>
