<p><ul><li>Vibrating Fork</li><li>Vibrating Rod</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vibration level switch market is primarily categorized into two types: vibrating fork and vibrating rod switches. Vibrating fork switches utilize a tuning fork mechanism that oscillates at a specific frequency, detecting changes in liquid levels by sensing variations in vibration patterns. Vibrating rod switches function similarly but use a single rod that vibrates to sense the presence or absence of material. Both types are widely used in industrial applications for their reliability and accuracy in level measurement across various substances.</p></p>
