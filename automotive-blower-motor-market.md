<p><ul><li>Brushed Motor</li><li>Brushless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive blower motor market is primarily categorized into brushed and brushless motors. Brushed motors utilize carbon brushes for commutation, offering a simple and cost-effective design, commonly found in older vehicles. In contrast, brushless motors employ electronic controllers for efficient operation, providing higher reliability, longer lifespan, and better energy efficiency, making them increasingly popular in modern vehicles. Both types serve critical roles in climate control systems, impacting passenger comfort and overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
