<p><p>The Temporomandibular Joint (TMJ) implant market includes two primary components: the mandibular component and the fossa component. The mandibular component is designed to replace or support the lower jaw, ensuring proper movement and alignment. Conversely, the fossa component serves as the upper joint structure, providing a stable surface for the mandibular component to articulate with. These components work together to alleviate pain, restore function, and improve the quality of life for patients suffering from TMJ disorders.</p></p>
