<p><p>The humerus compression plate market is divided into two main types: distal plates and proximal plates. Distal plates are designed for use in the lower region of the humerus, aiding in the stabilization of fractures and enabling effective healing by providing secure fixation. Proximal plates, on the other hand, are utilized for the upper region of the humerus, particularly in fractures near the shoulder. Both types play crucial roles in orthopedic procedures, enhancing recovery and overall patient outcomes.</p></p>
