<p><p>The renewable polyethylene market is categorized into three main types based on density and properties: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for its strength and durability, making it ideal for rigid containers and piping. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) offers flexibility and tensile strength, commonly used in films and bags. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is characterized by its low density and is often employed in flexibility-demanding applications like plastic wraps. Each type caters to specific industry needs while promoting sustainability.</p></p>
