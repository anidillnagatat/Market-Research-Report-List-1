<p><p>Cold chamber die casting machines are categorized into manual and semi-automatic types, each serving distinct operational needs. Manual machines require operators to perform most functions, offering lower initial costs but increased labor intensity. In contrast, semi-automatic machines automate key processes such as metal pouring and ejection, enhancing efficiency and precision while reducing labor requirements. Both types cater to various production scales, with manual machines suitable for small runs and semi-automatic types favored for larger, more consistent production needs.</p></p>
